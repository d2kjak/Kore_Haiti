KORE Haiti Inc is a 501 ©3 organization founded in 2015 by three Haitian-born medical students who shared a common passion for providing HUMANISTIC care to communities in Haiti. Since its inception, the organization has been addressing the HEALTH DISPARITIES associated with chronic and preventable diseases by focusing on the social determinant factors that influences their prevalence. Our COMMUNITY-BASED approach of identifying and addressing the burden associated with health care in local communities has resulted in three mission trips since inception with over 1450 patients screened and treated. Additionally, many more patients have been educated through our culturally-conscious and interactive EDUCATION sessions. Our partnership with local clinics and organization ensures adequate follow-up and LONGITUDINAL care for patients in our communities.

What We Do

Partnerships

  • Link communities and non-profit organizations with each other to maximize support.

  • Collaborate with political, spiritual, and educational leaders within the community to strategically address healthcare disparities in a unique fashion for each community.

  • Utilize established community resources to develop innovative healthcare strategies through a multi-faceted approach.
Mission Trips

  • Provide resources for a streamlined process for volunteering in Haiti.

  • Research the needs of vulnerable communities in Haiti to provide necessary healthcare, education, and economic services during medical mission trips.
Sponsorships

  • Initiate community projects that align with our mission.

  • Provide a platform for passionate companies to contribute direct care to Haitian communities.
