Donate


Support is critical to the success of KORE Haiti, Inc. During our medical mission trip to Cap- Haitien in March 2015, we made an enormous impact in the community. Our successful trip was made possible thanks to the kind donations of medical supplies, services, and financial resources by our volunteers, friends, and sponsors. All donations and financial resources are used directly to benefit communities we serve. Donations can be tagged as either general or designated to one of our projects. General donation are used to cover costs of ongoing projects, administrative fees, or needs at the discretion of KORE Haiti, Inc. Designated donations will be applied directly to a specific project at your request.

Thank you for your interest in supporting our commitment to the people of Haiti.
